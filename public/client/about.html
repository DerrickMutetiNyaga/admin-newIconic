Company Overview

Iconic Fibre is a national Internet Service Provider (ISP) in Kenya, offering wireless broadband to low-income and rural & urban communities. We provide quality data usage with high speeds and undisturbed coverage, enabling subscribers to access improved quality internet connections at low-cost prices.

Our hotspot packages are a unique way of competing in the existing market. The low-cost internet packages work favorably with our clients, with no need for installation fees. This has enabled a wider customer service reach.

We are constantly growing and expanding our coverage to serve more communities across Kenya. Our aim is to provide quality and reliable internet services that are extremely low cost to cater to our customer needs.
